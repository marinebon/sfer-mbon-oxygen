Interpolated field map

The map below is interactive: pan and zoom to explore the gridded dissolved oxygen field. Use Prev / Next in the right-side panel to step through depth bands, and adjust the O₂ color scale limits in the same panel. CTD observations are colored with the same palette as the interpolated field.
